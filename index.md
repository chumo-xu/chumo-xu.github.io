## About Me

I am a Research Assistant at Westlake University, supervised by Prof. [Chi Zhang](https://icoz69.github.io/).

I received my MRes degree from Hamlyn Centre for Robotic Surgery, Imperial College London in 2025, supervised by Prof. [Daniel Elson](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=SnuIIlsAAAAJ&hl=en) and Prof. [Baoru Huang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=unbPvWAAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN). I received my Bachelor's degree from Harbin Institute of Technology, Weihai in 2024.

My current research focuses on **3D/4D reconstruction**, **generative models**, **video generation**, and **world models**.

I sometimes write notes and longer posts on [**my blog**](https://chumo-xu.github.io/blog/).
